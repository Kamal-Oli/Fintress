Fintress is a financial intelligence platform that helps small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs)
detect early signs of financial pressure and understand their financial health through clear,
data-driven insights.

## What Fintress Does
- Identifies early financial stress signals
- Analyses liquidity, leverage, and profitability
- Converts financial data into plain-language insights
- Supports better decision-making for non-financial founders

Fintress is not accounting software.
It does not provide financial advice or predictions.
It is designed to support awareness and understanding of financial health.
